"She wasn't petal open anymore with him" - Page 110 (PDF)

Perhaps, the flower in this image is Janie. The petals on the flower seem to be closing in, alluding to the idea that "she wasn't petal open anymore." Jody and Janie's marriage is unhappy and Janie feels as if she cannot talk to her husband anymore. The surroundings of the flower seemed to be dark, void of happiness. Inside the flower, though, there seems to be a sense of light, a sense of happiness. Even if Janie is not aware of it, there is love within Janie. But, she can't demonstrate in a passionless, sad marriage. She has not found her true love and so she is unable to project her love onto others. She is forced to close the light that exists within her and wait until it is the right season to open her petals once again.
