""Ah wants things sweet wid mah marriage lak when you sit under a pear tree and think..." - Page 56 (PDF)

The model captures and represents the quote well. We can see the pear tree and the pears in the image. We can also see that the pears are "sweet," given their ripeness in color. We also see the idea of light and the pear tree reappear in this image. The pear tree in the previous image contained many features that we found to be light. The pear tree in this image has light focused on the pears themselves. We established that light equates to happiness, which Janie finds to be love. Janie wishes for this happiness, given her usage of the word "want." Love, to Janie, is like the pear tree in the image: full of happiness and light. She does not have that type of happiness at that moment though, given her unhappy relationship with Logan Killicks. It was a marriage that came with security but did not come with happiness.
