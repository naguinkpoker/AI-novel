"The kiss of his memory made pictures of love and light against the wall" - Page 242 (PDF)

This image shows Janie's wall of memories, which includes memories with Logan, Jody, and Tea Cake. The majority of the wall is dark, covered with many memories of her life. Not to say that she was completely unhappy with them, but she simply did not find love. Her memories with Logan and Jody are dark, signifying that they are rather unhappy and lack feelings related to love. Tea Cake's memories, however, "made pictures of love and light against the wall." Tea Cake's memories on the wall are glowing. Janie had achieved true happiness through Tea Cake, thus fulfilling her definition of true love. It was only after her third marriage that Janie was happy. Tea Cake was dead but his memories would continue to glow and stand out to her.
