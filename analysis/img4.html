"Dis is uh love game. Ah done lived Grandma's way now Ah means tuh live mine" - Page 158 (PDF)

This image places focus on the character, Janie. The other aspects of the image are given a 'bokeh' effect. Perhaps, the 'bokeh' effect speaks to the uncertainty that Janie has in leaving Logan for Jody. The 'bokeh' effect also speaks to the uncertainty that Janie has to live her life through her jurisdiction, not Nanny's. It is game-like in a sense; Janie is about to leave the first level and move on to the next: "Dis is uh love game." The next level is unclear. It is also interesting that we see the idea of light reappeared. Janie seems to be in a place that is not as well-lit, alluding to the idea that she is not happy in her current marriage. The next level though, the one that contains Jody Starks in it, does contain light. Janie, in the image, seems ready to move on to the next level, one with happiness but uncertainty.
