Image Represenations of Their Eyes Were Watching God

Using a modified version of OpenAI’s DALL-E machine learning model, we created image representations of Zora Neale Hurston’s Their Eyes Were Watching God. We do so by utilizing pieces of text as inputs into the model. More specifically, we use pieces of text that capture Janie, the main protagonist of the novel, and her experiences with love. These quotes aim to demonstrate not only what Janie finds to be love but also how Janie experiences love. Each quote is not chosen based on its ability to generate the best images but rather its ability to provide us with the closest answers to our questions. In doing so, we try to question and answer: what is love to Janie? Do we see Janie’s definition of love change over time? How do her experiences affect that definition? Our interest in this project stems from a genuine interest in the DALL-E model and the novel itself. The images presented below are simply one way to represent the text in this book. Machine learning is, to a degree, unpredictable and various predictions and outputs can be made from the same piece of text. We also understand that there are a plethora of quotes that we can use to talk about love in the context of Janie. For this project and the resources, we have available to us, we decided to create 9 images. Our modified DALL-E model has been trained for several hours on different pieces of text, for thousands of iterations. Below, you will find the result of many training sessions and their respective text inputs. Once you click on an image, you will be guided to a separate page that contains the image’s respective analysis.

Our complete analysis of this work has allowed us to understand that happiness is love for Janie. She ends her chase for love when she finds happiness with Tea Cake.
